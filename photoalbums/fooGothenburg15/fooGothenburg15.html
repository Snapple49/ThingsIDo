Foo Fighters Gothenburg 2015

So I was to the Foo Fighters concert in Gothenburg this June. All I have to say is Holy Crap. Most of the Foo fans out there have probably heard by now that Dave Grohl broke his leg during the show, just the second song in (Monkey Wrench, anyone wondering). Then he came back and finished the show with a broken leg! Crazy! I consider myself very lucky to have been to this show, not because it was great to see a guy break his leg. It was really awful, he tweeted photos of the x-ray of a really messed up leg, poor guy. But the atmosphere of the show and in the crowd when Dave was carried back onto stage, sat with his new friend and paramedic holding his leg together, and just rocked the hell out of the stadium, was just magical. Hands down best show I've ever been to. You rock, Dave! Here's a few of the photos I took, it was a crazy big audience as well!
